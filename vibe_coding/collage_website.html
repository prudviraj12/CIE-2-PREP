Who We Are

About MLRIT

Marri Laxman Reddy Institute of Technology (MLRIT) is a premier engineering institution located in Dundigal, Hyderabad, Telangana. Established in 2007, the college is affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Hyderabad (JNTUH) and approved by AICTE, New Delhi.

MLRIT is committed to imparting quality technical education and holistic development of students. The institute fosters innovation, research, and entrepreneurship through its state-of-the-art infrastructure and experienced faculty.

The campus spans over 10 acres of lush greenery providing a serene and productive learning environment. MLRIT has consistently produced top engineers who are making a difference in global industries.

Grading System

Academic Excellence

JNTUH Credit & Grading System

MLRIT follows the JNTUH-prescribed Credit Based Grading System (CBGS) for all B.Tech programs. The evaluation includes internal assessments, mid-exams, and semester-end exams.

Each semester has mid-term examinations held twice, carrying 30 marks each. The semester-end exam carries 70 marks, totaling 100 marks per subject.

Attendance Requirement: Minimum 75% attendance is mandatory to appear for semester exams. Condonation up to 65% is permitted for valid reasons. MLRIT ensures regular lab sessions, seminars, and industrial visits count toward credits.
CGPA Calculation: CGPA = Σ(Credits × Grade Points) ÷ Total Credits. Minimum CGPA of 5.0 required to pass. Distinction awarded for CGPA ≥ 7.5.

How to Apply

1

Appear for TS EAMCET / JEE

Qualify the state or national level entrance exam conducted by TSCHE or NTA.

2

Web Counselling

Register and participate in TS EAMCET web counselling process, exercising college and branch preferences.

3

Document Verification

Report to college with original documents: 10th, Intermediate marks memos, Caste certificate, and residence proof.

4

Fee Payment & Enrollment

Pay the semester fee and complete enrollment formalities to confirm admission.

5

Orientation & Classes Begin

Attend orientation program and commence academic journey at MLRIT.
